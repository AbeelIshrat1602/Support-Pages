What is primary care, and why is it important?

Primary care serves as the initial point of contact within the healthcare system, offering comprehensive services aimed at maintaining and improving health. It encompasses preventive care, diagnosis and treatment of acute and chronic illnesses, and health education. Establishing a relationship with a primary care provider (PCP) ensures continuity of care and early detection of potential health issues.

What primary care services does Nao Medical offer?

Nao Medical provides a wide range of primary care services, including:
  • Preventive Care: Routine check-ups, immunizations, and screenings.
  • Chronic Disease Management: Ongoing care for conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, and asthma.
  • Acute Illness Treatment: Care for common illnesses like colds, flu, and infections.
  • Women's Health Services: Gynecological exams, family planning, and prenatal care.
  • Pediatric Care: Health services for infants, children, and adolescents.
  • Mental Health Services: Counseling and management of mental health conditions.

How can I schedule an appointment with a primary care provider at Nao Medical?

Appointments can be scheduled by visiting Nao Medical's website or by calling the nearest location directly. Same-day appointments and walk-in visits are also accommodated to ensure timely access to care.

Does Nao Medical accept my insurance plan?

Nao Medical accepts most major insurance plans. To confirm whether your specific plan is accepted, please contact Nao Medical directly or consult your insurance provider.

What should I bring to my primary care appointment?

Please bring the following to your appointment:
  • A valid photo ID (e.g., driver's license, passport).
  • Your insurance card.
  • A list of current medications, including dosages.
  • Any relevant medical records or test results.
  • A list of questions or concerns you wish to discuss with your provider.

Can Nao Medical's primary care providers refer me to specialists if needed?

Yes, if specialized care is necessary, Nao Medical's primary care providers can refer you to appropriate specialists within their network to ensure coordinated and comprehensive care.

Are telemedicine services available for primary care at Nao Medical?

Nao Medical offers telemedicine services, allowing patients to consult with primary care providers remotely for certain health concerns. This service enhances accessibility and convenience for patients.

How do I update my primary care provider (PCP) with my insurance plan to Nao Medical?

To designate a Nao Medical provider as your PCP, contact your insurance company and request the change. They will guide you through the process to ensure your records are updated accordingly.

For more information or specific inquiries, please contact Nao Medical directly or visit their website.

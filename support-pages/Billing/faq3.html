Can I Get a Receipt to Submit to My Insurance?

Yes, after a visit to Nao Medical, we make it easy for you to obtain a receipt to submit to your insurance. You can do so through multiple channels.

How to Obtain a Receipt:

  • Patient Portal: Many healthcare providers, including Nao Medical, offer patient portals where you can access and download your medical records, including receipts. Check your portal for the option to download or print your receipt.
  • Contact Nao Medical Directly: Reach out to Nao Medical's customer service or billing department to request a copy of your receipt. They can email or mail the receipt to you upon your request.
  • Check Payment Confirmation: If you paid for your visit online or through the Nao Medical app, you might find a detailed receipt in your email inbox or in the app itself.

Important Considerations:

  • Insurance Coverage: It's important to check your specific insurance provider's requirements for submitting claims. Some insurance providers may require specific forms or detailed documentation.
  • Itemized Receipt: If your insurance company requires an itemized receipt, request one from Nao Medical. This will provide a breakdown of each service you received.

By keeping clear communication with Nao Medical and your insurance provider, you can ensure that you have all the necessary documentation to successfully submit your claim. If you have any questions, our team is always available to assist you.

Understanding healthcare billing can feel complicated, but this section simplifies everything, guiding you step-by-step toward solutions.

From accessing your billing statements to understanding charges and payment options, we empower you to manage billing efficiently and stress-free.

How much does it cost for children aged 14 and below?

The price starts at $175.

What is included in the $129 price?

  • Your visits
  • Completion of Form I693
  • Civil surgeon visit

How much does it cost for individuals aged 15 and above?

The price starts at $149.

What is included in the $149 price?

  • Your visits
  • Completion of Form I693
  • Civil surgeon visit
  • Syphilis test (RPR) and blood draw

What other costs should I expect?

This will be determined by a medical professional based on your records and any additional vaccinations or labs needed.

How long does the process take?

On average, it takes 7-10 days.

Can I complete the process in 1-2 days?

Expedited processing is available for an additional $150, but this depends on whether all your labs and vaccinations are already completed.

How many visits will I need to complete the process?

Typically, two visits:
  • Visit 1: Testing.
  • Visit 2: Reviewing results and evaluation by the civil surgeon.
Additional visits may be required if labs, vaccinations, or treatments are needed.

What documents should I bring to my appointment?

  • Passport or government-issued ID.
  • Vaccination records.
  • Lab results from the past year.

How can I get to the Hicksville location?

By Bus: From Queens, take the eastbound N22 bus from Hillside Ave and Springfield Blvd towards Hicksville LIRR Station. Nao Medical is 8 blocks south of Old Country Road.

By Train: From Penn Station, take the LIRR towards Ronkonkoma and alight at Hicksville station. Walk 4 blocks south on Newbridge Road towards Old Country Road. Taxis are also available at the station for a 2-minute ride.

What are the transportation options for the Astoria location?

By Car: Take Grand Central Parkway and exit at 31st Street (last exit before Triboro Bridge).

By Bus: Take the Q69 or Q101 bus to the intersection of Steinway Street and Ditmars Boulevard, or the Q19 or M60 to Steinway St.

By Subway: From Manhattan, take the N or W train to the last stop, Ditmars Boulevard. We're 2 blocks southeast of the station.

Where is the Bartow location in the Bronx, and how do I get there?

Address: Bartow Mall, a plaza that also houses Dollar Tree and Arby's, between Asch Loop and Bartow Ave.

By Bus: Accessible via Bx23, Bx26, Bx28, Bx29, Bx30, Bx38, Bx12 SBS, and others.

By Subway: Take the #5 subway to Gun Hill Road, then transfer to a bus heading towards Bartow Mall.

What landmarks are near the 174th Street, Bronx location?

Located between East 174th Street Pharmacy and Metro PCS, across from Western Union, between Southern Blvd and Hoe Avenue.

Is parking available at Nao Medical locations?

Parking Availability:

  • Hicksville: Parking available.
  • Astoria: Street parking only.
  • Bartow, Bronx: Parking available.
  • 174th Street, Bronx: Street parking only.
  • Brooklyn: Street parking only.

How can I reach the Brooklyn location by public transport?

The Brooklyn location is accessible via the Franklin Avenue Subway Station at the corner of Franklin Avenue and Eastern Parkway, served by the 2, 3, 4, and 5 subway trains.

Are Nao Medical locations open on holidays?

Yes, all locations are open on holidays with operating hours from 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM.

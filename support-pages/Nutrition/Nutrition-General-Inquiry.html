What services do Nao Medical's nutritionists provide?

Our nutritionists offer personalized meal plans, nutritional assessments, dietary counseling, education on balanced nutrition, weight management strategies, disease-specific dietary guidance, and ongoing support to help you achieve and maintain optimal health.

How can a nutritionist help me manage a specific health condition?

Our nutritionists develop specialized meal plans and provide guidance tailored to conditions such as diabetes, heart disease, or food allergies, helping you manage your health through proper nutrition.

What should I expect during my initial consultation?

The initial consultation includes a thorough assessment of your health status, dietary habits, and lifestyle to understand your unique needs and goals.

Are the nutrition services covered by insurance?

We accept all major insurance plans. For specific coverage details, please contact your insurance provider or our office.

Do you offer virtual nutrition consultations?

Yes, we provide tele-nutrition or virtual care options, allowing you to receive expert nutritional guidance from the comfort of your home.

How often will I need to meet with the nutritionist?

The frequency of visits depends on your individual needs and goals. Our nutritionists will work with you to establish a schedule that supports your progress.

What qualifications do Nao Medical's nutritionists have?

Our nutritionists are licensed professionals with extensive experience in providing personalized nutrition counseling and support.

How can I schedule an appointment with a Nao Medical nutritionist?

You can book an appointment online through our website or call our office directly. We offer flexible scheduling, including evening and weekend appointments, to accommodate your needs.

What are the costs for services without insurance?

For patients without health insurance, the cost for a walk-in urgent care visit at Nao Medical is $175. This is significantly lower than the average cost of $280 at other accredited urgent care facilities in the New York area.

How does billing work with health insurance?

If you have health insurance, your costs may include copayments and deductibles as determined by your specific plan. It's important to understand your plan's details to avoid unexpected expenses. We accept all major insurance plans.

What is a copay?

A copay, or copayment, is a fixed fee you pay at the time of receiving medical services. This amount is predetermined by your health insurance plan and typically applies to services like doctorâ€™s appointments, prescriptions, or diagnostic tests.

What is a deductible?

A deductible is the amount you must pay out-of-pocket for covered medical expenses before your insurance company begins to share the costs.

Does Nao Medical offer telemedicine services?

Yes, for minor illnesses or injuries, telemedicine offers a convenient and cost-effective alternative to in-person visits.

How can I avoid unexpected medical bills?

To prevent unexpected expenses: know your copay, ask for upfront pricing, consider telemedicine, and choose independent urgent care centers.
